Let’s keep our kids safe on social media by teaching them how to be responsible digitial citizens.

overview

We believe the best way to teach kids how to be digital citizens is to let them be digital cizitens - but in a safe, protected environment.

Using the Pryvy Social app, kids can learn how to use social media responsibly by posting pictures, sharing updates & commenting.

How it works

Teachers sign up for Pryvy online, and create a Pryvy group for their class. For example, “Ms. White 2015”.

Parents create a Pryvy account for their kids, and add their kids to the class group.

Everyone in the group can start using the Pryvy Social app to share updates and photos, and post comments.

Protected

Only teachers can invite other people to the group, and can remove people at any time.

Private

Any thoughts or photos posted by either kids or adults in the group can only be seen by other group members.

Free

The Pryvy Social app is complety free for both teachers & parents. Other Pryvy apps have optional ungrade features for parents.

No 1-1 Convos

Group members can only communicate as a group, and can not have 1-1 conversations unless a direct connection is made outside of the group.

Accessible

The Pryvy Social app is available on iPhone/iPad, as well as on any Chrome browser on any device.

Not "for kids"

Pryvy Social isn’t childish, and works just like other social apps with the ability to add filters to photos, or draw pictures.

Take a look

Your class' Pryvy Social page is a place for kids to post pictures, drawings and class updates to the group.

Parents & teachers can also participate by posting comments.
