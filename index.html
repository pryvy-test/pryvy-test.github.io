Why Pryvy?

Protect

Social media is no place for a child, yet every year younger and younger kids start signing up. With Pryvy, everyone gets what they want - kids can be social online with their friends, and parents can rest easy knowing strangers and mature content have to go through them first.

Teach

It's time to start teaching kids about social media like we teach them to drive - with practice, not on a chalkboard.

Your group can use Pryvy as part of a formal or informal social media education program. Either way, kids have the opportunity to learn how to use social media responsibly and respectfully.

Connect

Allow your group to stay connected even when you're not together. Pryvy not only allows kids to stay in the loop about schedule changes, they can also strengthen the bonds between each other.

How it works

Create a group

Adults sign up for Pryvy, and create a group.

Invite Members

Parents sign up, and create log ins for their kids. Both parents & kids can join the group.

Share & chat

Download the apps to connect with your group.

Features

Parents control contacts

Parents decide who is allowed to contact their child and who is blocked. Parents can approve individual people or entire groups.

Photos & thoughts are private

Everything shared or sent on Pryvy will only ever be seen by approved friends and family - not strangers and not advertisers.

Not a popularity contest

Likes, retweets, followers, friends, comments, favorites... It's dizzying. For kids, this constant popularity contest leads to anxiety, compulsive behavior and social media addiction. With Pryvy, kids can interact with each others posts, but nothing is tallied.

Accessible

The Pryvy apps are available on iOS devices, Android devices, and any device with a Chrome browser.
