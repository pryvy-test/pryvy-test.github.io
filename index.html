Why Pryvy?

We believe kids can be social online and stay safe. Pryvy does this by making parents the gatekeepers. They decide who is allowed to contact their child, and who is blocked.

Features

No Stranger Danger

Parents can rest easy knowing their kids can not meet or exchange information with strangers.

Complete Privacy

Pryvy's social network is completely private, which means any thoughts or pictures your child shares will only ever be seen by parent-approved friends & family.

Group communication

Create a group for a sports team, class, scout troop or any other group for easy communication between group members - both adults & kids. Learn more »

Cut off cyberbullies

With just a few clicks parents can completely cut off cyberbullies, ensuring they can no longer communicate with their child on any Pryvy app.
