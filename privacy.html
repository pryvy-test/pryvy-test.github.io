Privacy Policy

Updated: March 11, 2014

Pryvy ("Pryvy") is operated and presented to users in all locations by the Pryvy LLC, a Nevada limited liability company (the “Company”). This Privacy Policy covers use of information collected by the Company.

We are committed to providing a fun, entertaining, and safe website and applications for people of all ages. We are dedicated to safeguarding any personal information collected on the Pryvy website (www.pryvy.com) and/or Pryvy applications (collectively, the “Pryvy System”). Our Privacy Policy and our information practices adhere to the United States Children's Online Privacy Protection Act.

To help ensure a rewarding online experience for our visitors, we provide you with this Privacy Policy that describes the information practices of the Pryvy System Pryvy website (www.pryvy.com) and/or the Pryvy application(s) (collectively, the “System”), including how we collect, use and disclose personal information.

The Information We Collect   We require consent from a parent or a legal guardian of a child (a “Parent”) when a child wants to be registered for the Pryvy System. Only a Parent may create a Pryvy account for a child. Unless a Parent creates an account for a child, a child may not use the Pryvy System. A child may initiate the registration process by using the Pryvy System to send an email to the Company with the child’s first name and a valid email address for the child’s Parent. The Pryvy System will then automatically email an invitation to register for the Pryvy System to the Parent without retaining either the child’s name or the Parent’s email address. Alternatively, the Parent can directly initiate the registration process through the Pryvy System. As described in this Privacy Policy, we directly collect information only from the Parent during our initial registration process, including the Parent's email address, the Parent's account name, and the child's password for each registered user under the Parent’s account. When a Pryvy Parent registrant acquires a paid membership, we may collect additional personally identifiable information ("PII") from the Parent. The types of PII that may be collected at that time include the Parent's full name, email address, and credit card (or other payment method) billing information including mailing address. The Pryvy System does not condition a child's participation in an online activity on the child providing more personal information than is reasonably necessary for that activity. The Pryvy System requires a Parent to provide a valid email address in order to register on the System, and register a child under the Parent's Pryvy account before the child can participate in the Pryvy virtual world by sending or receiving telephone, email or text messages, playing games and viewing content. We use the Parent’s email address to send the Parent a message that can be used to activate the Pryvy account, to notify the Parent about our privacy practices, and to send the Parent communications either about the Parent’s and child’s Pryvy accounts or about features of our Pryvy System, including periodic Parent newsletters about Pryvy, and for such other purposes as described in this Privacy Policy. This email address is correlated with the account user name. With the Parent’s prior permission, we also may use this email address to send the Parent promotional messages regarding Pryvy products and services. The Parent may revoke their permission to receive promotional messages regarding Pryvy at any time by emailing customer support at support@pryvy.com and may unsubscribe from promotional email communications from Pryvy by clicking on unsubscribe links in those emails. We may also request a user name and email address when a user submits a form through the "Contact Us" portion of the Pryvy System so that we may respond to the inquiry. We collect the e-mail address for the sole purpose of responding directly on a one-time basis to a specific inquiry. For some of our online activities - such as polls, surveys, games or contests - we may ask users to provide information that is not personally identifiable, such as country of residence or a user’s favorite color. We also enable users to chat online through our Pryvy System, but only to those Pryvy users you have pre-approved. Nonetheless, if you use the chat function within the Pryvy System and disclose personally identifiable information, you should be aware that such information could be read, collected or used by other approved Pryvy users. We strongly encourage you not to disclose personal information (such as real name, email address, telephone number, etc.) about the user or about any third party in such chats and are not responsible for the personally identifiable information you choose to submit through the chat function.
