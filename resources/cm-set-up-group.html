Set up your group

1. Create a Pryvy account at app.pryvy.com/signup

To protect the kids on Pryvy you will be asked to verify both an email address and a phone number.

2. Create your group

Note: Your group username should be all lowercase with no spaces.

3. Invite parents to Pryvy

Send an email to parents letting them know to sign up for Pryvy and join your group.

You can also attach this PDF overview. Pryvy Overview for Parents »

Here is a sample email:

Hello parents!

Our child ministry has decided to start using Pryvy group communication apps, which are built specifically for groups of kids. The reason for doing so is 3-fold:

1. We can strengthen the bonds within our child ministry by sharing & chatting even when we're not together.
2. We can protect kids by giving them a private and protected alternative to adult social apps.
3. We can use these apps to teach kids how to use adult social media apps responsibly when the time comes.

I've already created a group called MY GROUP NAME. To join, sign up for Pryvy as a family at pryvy.com, then request to join this group on behalf of yourself and your kids.

Please let know if you have any questions!

4. Accept requests to join your group

Once parents get their family set up on Pryvy, they will send you a request to join your group. You will be notified of each new request via email.

5. You're all set!

Once everyone has joined your group you can start using the Pryvy apps to share & chat.
