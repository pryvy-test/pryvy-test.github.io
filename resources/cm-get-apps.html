Help Kids Get Started

Print out a guide for iPhone, iPod or iPad users »

Print out a guide for Chrome browser users »

The next time your group meets, share a post with your group, and walk kids through how to make a post of their own.

Before kids leave, ask them to share a fun activity over the next week. It can be anything from a picture from a soccer game, or helping their parents make breakfast. Try to share something yourself so kids get a feel for how it works.
