Lesson 1 Summary: Have fun with social

Social apps are supposed to be fun. But if kids aren't careful, these apps can be the cause of unnessesary anxiety & obsession. Kids need to be taught from day 1 that number of comments these get, or the number of friends they have really doesn't matter.

Lesson 2 Summary: Share like the world is watching

All adults know what viral sharing is, but it's not intuitive to kids. As they are starting out, they need to be told that everything they share online has the potential to be shared with people they don't expect.

Lesson 3 Summary: Be careful who your friends are

Kids are naturally trusting, which isn't the best trait when dealing with strangers online. As a rule of thumb, kids should only connect someone if their parents would allow them to hang out with that person alone.

Lesson 4 Summary: Show respect in all ways & always

Adults know how easy it is for online conversations to become hateful and mean. We can teach the next generation to keep the internet a pleasant place by teaching them that words spoken are not less hurtful than words spoken face-to-face.

Lesson 5 Summary: Take a stand against cyber-bullying

Cyber-bullying has become the source of far too much pain for kids and teens. It's time to start teaching kids to take a stand again bullying

Lesson 6 Summary: Protect your privacy

As kids are getting started, they might be tempted to share personal information unknowingly. These are things like their address, their grades or maybe photos of themselves breaking rules.
