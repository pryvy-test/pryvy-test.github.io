What is Pryvy?

Pryvy is a set of 4 kid-safe communication apps for text messaging, voice calling, social sharing & email. With Pryvy, parents pre-approve all their child's connections so there is no risk of strangers, bullies or scammers.

How it works

Sign up online and add create logins for your kids.

Download the Pryvy Messages and Pryvy Phone apps on your family's devices.

Your family can start texting & calling each other using the apps. When your child tries to connect with someone new on Pryvy, you'll be asked to approve the connection first.
