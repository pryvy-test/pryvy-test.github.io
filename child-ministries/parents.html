How Pryvy Keeps Kids Safe

Parents control contacts

Parents decide who is allowed to contact their child and who is blocked. Parents can approve individual people or entire groups.

Photos & thoughts are private

Everything shared or sent on Pryvy will only ever be seen by approved friends and family - not strangers and not advertisers.

Why does this matter?

Kids are flocking to social media apps by the millions, and often behind our backs.

These apps aren't meant for kids, and don't come with parental controls.

Top 5 Social Media Dangers

Social media not only poses physical dangers, it can also corrode your child's Christian values.

Sexting has become an epedemic

Kids are growing faster than ever, and sexting has become an epidemic. Unfortunately, the consequences of sexting can be disastrous.

Pervasive Profanity

Social media doesn't come with a filter, which means kids are exposed to profanity, talk of drug use and aggressive language.

Constant contact with strangers

Comment boards, online gaming and video chat all help kids stay connected, but they also put kids in direct contact with total strangers.

Missteps can lead to expulsion or even jail time

Kids make mistakes all the time, and even saying something jokingly can permenantly damage your child's future.

Bullying is a constant torment

Before social media, bulling was limited to school hours, but now bullying is a non-stop torment for a lot of kids.

The bottom line: We need to give kids a safe alternative to traditional social media apps.

How to Get Started

Signup

Create a free account at app.pryvy.com, then create logins for your kids.

Join the group

Your Pastor or Child Ministry leader will create a group, and will manage all members. To join the group, search for your church, and send your group leader a request to join on behalf of your kids.

Get the apps

Once you and your child are members of the group, you can use the Pryvy apps to share & send messages to everyone else in the group.

Pryvy's apps are available on all iOS devices & Chrome browsers.
