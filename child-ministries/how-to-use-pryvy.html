STRENGTHEN THE BONDS WITHIN YOUR CHILD MINISTRY

Share photos & updates

App to use: Pryvy Social

Group members can share personal photos, event photos, updates and other news.

Other group members can add comments or even add additional photos.

Parents maintain visibility into what is being shared and a can easily monitor activity and remove objectionable content.

Teach & Engage

App to use: Pryvy Social

Youth leaders can engage group members outside the classroom or group events to reinforce what is being taught in an easy, familiar and comfortable environment.

Leaders can ask questions about recent teachings, share scripture or even encourage them to share prayer requests.

Encourage

App to use: Pryvy Social

Use the Pryvy Social app to post encouragements to specific members or the entire group.

Kids & teens can post encouraging statements to help each other through the challenges of growing up.

This helps build relationships and provides much needed support during this critical stage of growth and maturity.

Make Communication Easy

Free group messaging

App to use: Pryvy Social

Pryvy Social

Currently kids under the age of 13 have no easy way of communicating with each other or their youth group leaders.

With Pryvy’s free group messaging feature, your entire Child Ministry can send group messages.

It’s perfect for asking questions, notifying kids about event details, time changes and coordinating group events.

PROTECT KIDS FROM ONLINE DANGERS

By giving them a safe alternative to adult social apps,

an online community where they can feel connected & loved,

and the opportunity to learn how to be responsible, respectful digital citizens as teenagers & adults.
