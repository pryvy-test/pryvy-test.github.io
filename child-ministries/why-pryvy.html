Why Use Pryvy?

There are 3 reasons to use Pryvy: Teach kids about social media, stay connected as a group, and protect kids by giving them an alternative to adult social apps.

Background

Kids are flocking to social media apps by the millions, and often behind our backs.

These apps aren't meant for kids, and don't come with parental controls.

Teach

The best way to keep kids safe online is to teach them how to be responsible digital citizens.

To friend or not to friend?

Making friends online isn't always black and white. Kids need to learn to evaluate every friend request, and should be aware that not everyone is who they say.

Think twice, share once

We all have stupid thoughts, and kids need to learn that not every thought belongs on social media. The consequences of making a wrong move can lead to expulsion, privacy violations or even jail time in the case of tween/teen sexting.

Respect, respect, respect

We see them everywhere: internet trolls and cyber-bullies. Perhaps the next generation can do better than we are if we teach them early and often about speaking respectfully online.

Connect

The Pryvy sharing & messaging apps allow your group to stay connected even when you're not together.

Share photos & updates

Kids, group leaders and parents can share personal photos, event photos, updates and other news using the Pryvy Social app.

Teach and Engage

Educators & group leaders can use the Pryvy Social app to reinforce recent lessons, and engage kids by asking questions.

Stay up-to-date

Unlike almost every other social network, photos and thoughts posted on Pryvy can only be seen by approved friends or groups, not strangers and not advertisers. Furthermore, we allow content to be completely deleted no questions asked.

Protect

By giving kids protected social & messaging apps that they can use with their friends, we can keep them off unsafe alternatives like Instagram & Snapchat.

Strangers, Scammers & Bullies

When kids & tweens join social networks, they are often targets of scammers and predators. With Pryvy, only parent approved groups or people can contact children.

Profanity & Nudity

Despite their best efforts, traditional social networks are overrun with profanity, nudity, drug use and hateful language. With Pryvy, kids can share with friends without being exposed to mature content.

Privacy

Unlike almost every other social network, photos and thoughts posted on Pryvy can only be seen by approved friends or groups, not strangers and not advertisers. Furthermore, we allow content to be completely deleted no questions asked.
