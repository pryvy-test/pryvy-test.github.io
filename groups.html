Pryvy Groups in a nutshell

Creating a group with Pryvy makes it easy to communicate across all the Pryvy apps.

Adults can create groups online, and become the group's admin.

Admins can invite both kids & adults to their group.

Parents approve or deny all group invitations sent to their kids.
